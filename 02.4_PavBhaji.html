Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients

For the Vada (Potato Filling):

  • Potatoes - 4 medium-sized (boiled and mashed)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste - 1 tsp
  • Green Chilies - 2-3 (finely chopped)
  • Turmeric Powder - ½ tsp
  • Curry Leaves - 8-10
  • Asafoetida (Hing) - a pinch
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - for tempering

For the Batter:

  • Besan (Gram Flour) - 1 cup
  • Turmeric Powder - ¼ tsp
  • Red Chili Powder - ½ tsp
  • Baking Soda - a pinch
  • Salt - to taste
  • Water - as needed
  • Oil - for frying

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare the Vada Filling :
    • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and asafoetida.
    • Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; sauté for a minute.
    • Add turmeric powder and mashed potatoes. Mix well.
    • Add salt and cook for 2-3 minutes. Let it cool and shape into small balls.
  2. Prepare the Batter:
    • In a bowl, mix besan, turmeric, red chili powder, salt, and baking soda.
    • Gradually add water to form a thick batter.
  3. Fry the Vadas:
    • Fry the Vadas
    • Dip each potato ball in the batter, coat evenly, and fry until golden brown.
    • Drain on a paper towel.
  4. Assemble the Vada Pav:
    • Slice the pav buns in half.
    • Spread green chutney on one side and tamarind chutney on the other.
    • Sprinkle dry garlic chutney.
    • Place a fried vada in the middle. Press gently to assemble.
    • Serve with fried green chilies.
